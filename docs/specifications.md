# Specifications

## Maximum Current Ratings

- USB-A Outputs: 1.5A
- USB-C Outputs: 5A (Detected as 3A by smart devices connected to it)
- Scew Terminals: Same as the maximum current rating for their specific votage output
- PG: Less than 20mA
